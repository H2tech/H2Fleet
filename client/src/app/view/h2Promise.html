H2 Promise through a proof of concept

If you are wondering how to make best use of the opportunities presented, what the value will be to your business and require the data to support your decision.

We provide you with the “H2 Promise through a proof of concept”.

The H2 Promise through a proof of concept allows us to provide the following service around our H2 Fuel cell.

1. We will send an engineer to your organisation to install a H2 fuel cell for each custom use case you may have – free of charge.

2. You can then run the H2 fuel cell for 14 days, which will give you the chance to collect your own data on the value / performance gains of the H2 fuel cell.

3. At the end of the 14 day proof of concept we can then discuss how you wish move forward.

The proof of concept is completely covered by our professional indemnity insurance.

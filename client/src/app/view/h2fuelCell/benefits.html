Engine health

Carbon build-up leads to potentially expensive issues such as expensive failures of Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valves, Diesel Particulate Filters (DPF filters), Turbo issues, inlet manifold and injector problems.

The H2 water fuel cell creates HHO molecules that pass through the air intake of an engine increasing the temperature of combustion and therefore stripping the built-up excess carbon that leads to poor running issues.

By removing harmful carbon build-up from your engine, you will experience both immediate and long term benefits, including. Cleaner engine and oils due to reduced hydrocarbon accumulation, which means fewer oil and filter changes.

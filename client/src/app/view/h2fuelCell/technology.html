The H2 Water Fuel cell uses energy from the carâ€™s battery to produce hydrogen & Oxygen gas from water in the HHO Cell. This gas is then added to fuel air mixture of the car before it enters the engine.

In diesel and petrol engined vehicles as much as 5 % of the fuel is not converted to useful energy and is instead lost mainly as heat. By adding HHO to the fuel air mixture a far cleaner burn of the fuel.

When utilising a HHO fuel cell far more of the fuel you pay for is converted into useful work to power the vehicle rather than being wasted as heat.

At the base level water is comprised of two atoms of hydrogen and one atom of Oxygen or H2O, which makes it perfect as a fuel source.

Our unique H2 fuel cell system will separate a water molecule into its component atoms, this is achieved by using very little energy and the subsequent gas is then introduce into the engine and oxidised as fuel.

The resulting energy is 2.5 times more powerful than petrol/diesel.

This totally changes the combustion process and the by-product of the combustion is water vapor. As a fuel, water is powerful, cheap and pollution free.

The outcome of which is that the improvement in emissions and miles per gallon far outweigh the energy required to produce the gas.

Of course you cannot fill your car with water yet, so for now we use this gas as a supplement to improve efficiency.

The H2 fuel cell aims to balance optimum performance with fuel economy.
